About Us

Welcome to Midnapore College Autonomous Saraswati Puja Committee! We are a dedicated team committed to celebrating and organizing the annual Saraswati Puja festivities at our esteemed college. Join us in fostering cultural vibrancy and academic spirit as we come together to worship the goddess of knowledge. Let the joyous celebration begin!.
